Warm-Up (5 mins) – “What is a Robot?” 👉 Ask: * Have you seen a robot before? * Where do we see robots? 👉 Tell them: * A robot is a machine that can do work * Robots help humans 👉 Activity: * Ask kids to act like robots 🤖 (walk stiff, say “beep beep”) --- ## 🟡 2. What Robots Need (5 mins) 👉 Teach: * Robots need electricity (power) to work * They do not eat food like humans 👉 Ask: * Do robots eat food? --- ## 🔵 3. Types of Robots Around Us (5 mins) 👉 Explain: * Home robots → cleaning * Factory robots → making things * Doctor robots → help in hospitals * Flying robots → drones 👉 Ask: * Which robot flies? --- ## 🟣 4. How Robots Work (5–7 mins) 👉 Teach: * Robots follow instructions * They have a brain (computer) * They use sensors (to see and feel) * They move using wheels or legs 👉 Activity: * Teacher gives command: “Raise your hand” * Kids follow → “You are acting like robots!” --- ## 🟠 5. What Robots Can & Cannot Do (5 mins) 👉 Robots can: * Work fast * Help humans * Clean and help doctors 👉 Robots cannot: * Eat food * Think like humans --- ## 🔴 6. Who Makes Robots? (3 mins) 👉 Teach: * Robots are made by humans called engineers 👉 Ask: * Do robots come from magic? --- ## 🟤 7. Quick Recap Game (3–5 mins) 👉 Ask rapid questions: * Robot needs? → Electricity * Robot follows? → Instructions * Robot can eat? → No * Flying robot? → Drone --- ## 🟢 8.
